Vert Pick-Up

Schedule time and location for your pick-up!

Our pick-up service lets you recycle your packages from the comfort of your own home. Just schedule when and where you'd like us to pick up, and we'll be right at your door!

Here's how it works:

  1. First, sign up for our services in order to track your donations and receive benefits. While signing up it will allow you to choose how often you want our service. We offer two options either every two weeks or monthly pickups. Sign up using the button below.
  2. Once you have received a package in the mail, separate the different components of the packaging into these different categories.
    • Cardboard and paper
    • Loose plastic and bubble wrap, plastic envelopes
    • Styrofoam
  3. As the pick up happens, the delivery worker will ask you to sign and they will count your items in order to log your donation. Once that's done your packaging is out of your hair!
  4. We will then ship back the used packaging to shipping companies in order to be reused.
