Vert Drop-Off

Visit one of our drop-off locations near you!

We are expanding our drop-off locations across the country. Easily find a location near you and drop off your package at your own convenience!

Here's how it works:

  1. Once you have received a package in the mail, separate the different components of the packaging into these different categories:
    • Cardboard and paper
    • Loose plastic and bubble wrap, plastic envelopes
    • Styrofoam
  2. Once you have your packaging separated, you can go to one of the drop off boxes at the closest location to you.
  3. At the box, you can punch in your username and password into the screen so that your donations can be tracked. The screen will ask you how many pieces of each component you have. After punching in the amount of each component, the drawer for that component will unlock and you are able to drop your packaging off! All of your donations will be logged in your account in order to receive benefits!
  4. We frequently empty our Vert bins in order to be shipped back to shipping companies in order to be reused.
